The Angler Fish

The Amazing Angler

The Angler fish lives in the depths of the ocean.
It makes its home in the darkness of abyss where sight is of no use and cold of night prevails.
When Angler fish mate, they melt into each other are share bodies forever, spending the rest of their days together as one.

Bioluminesence

Bioluminescence is the ability of an organism to create light. It is one the most amazing phenomena found in nature.
The phenomena is created with a reaction between an enzyme and a substrate, but very little is known about how the function is used.
Living in the depths of the sea, the Angler fish creates its own light via interal catalysts.
